OPM Film #1
A trip to Berlin and a meeting with Rainer Spehl. Rainer studied furniture design at the Royal College of Art in London from 1994 to 1999. Since 2003 he has been living in Berlin, where he is working independently for commercial and private clients. At the same time, Rainer regularly shows his own work in exhibitions around the world.
