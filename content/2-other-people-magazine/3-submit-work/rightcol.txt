We are interested in finding as many creatives and writers as possible. Get in touch by emailing (email: submit@other-people-magazine.com text: submit@other-people-magazine.com)

PHOTOGRAPHY
We are interested in photography that takes us on a journey, tells a story, let us travel and open our eyes.

WRITTEN 
We are interested in stories about people, inspirational. About changes, about a different approach to life, about passion, about overcoming challenges, about love, beauty, crafts, design, travel, art, food, smiles.
